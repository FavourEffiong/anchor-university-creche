What We Offer

AUL Creche opened its doors in 2015 with a unique vision to provide students with a globally focused study of arts and comprehensive child care services. We offer various programs tailored to different age groups and developmental stages.

Settling Program

Our settling program helps new children adjust to the creche environment gradually and comfortably. We work closely with parents to ensure a smooth transition.

  • Gradual introduction to creche environment
  • Personalized attention from dedicated staff
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Regular parent communication
